ABOUT

Line Models is a specialist Modelling Agency, based in Surry Hills, Sydney. We provide trained and professional fit models to designers and retailers in NSW, ACT, VIC and QLD.

Our company was established 2013 by an experienced fit-model and retailer team. Line was created to fill a niche market in Australia’s growing e-commerce and fashion industry. Line endeavours to develop and grow in the industry with focus and attention to each clients specific needs.

We aim to raise the bar for Australian fit models and the local online shopping experience with well-trained models and an uncompromised client-model relationship.
